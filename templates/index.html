Why Use This Self-Study Tool?

This tool has been designed by staff in the Finance department at Queen's University Belfast, using AI tools, specifically for our students. You are encouraged to use it for 30 minutes each day, to help you understand and remember your lecture notes more effectively.

It is powered by Artificial Intelligence to help you study and revise. AI is, and will be, an extremely helpful tool, but it should never be used for plagiarism, which would happen if you attempted to claim its output was your own work, without acknowledgement.

  • Upload your Lecture Notes - accepts PDFs and PowerPoint files of your QUB lecture notes to instantly create a personalized tutor
  • Key Concept Explanations - Get clear explanations of complex topics with real-world examples
  • Calculation Questions - Practice calculations with worked examples and step-by-step solutions
  • Interactive Quizzes - Test your knowledge with retrieval practice questions
  • Essay Practice - Generate comprehensive essay questions to develop analytical skills
  • Continuous Learning - Ask follow-up questions and dive deeper into any topic

Simply upload your notes in the sidebar to get started!
